# Metrieken
- Mean Squared Error
	- $$ MSE= 1 n∑ei 2 $$
- Root Mean Squared Error
	- $$ RMSE=√ 1 n∑ei 2 $$
- Mean Absolute Error
	- $$ MAE= 1 n∑|ei | $$
- Mean Absolute Percentage Error
	- $$ MAPE= 1 n∑| ei xi| $$
